TRAA WEBSITE BUILD

THE CLIENT

The Thames River Anglers Association is a grassroots organization that works to protect and populate local watersheds.
Our team was tasked with designing, and coding a responsive and fresh website for the client, taking the content and information from an existing site.

REQUIREMENTS

The team was required to make this website design responsive, from mobile to desktop, and included multiple Javascript enhancements such as a gallery slideshow and custom data charts. We also worked to produce an original promotional video for the client outlining their organization for use on social media.

THE FINAL RESULT

Our teams hard work and dedication to user experience resulted in a clean, modern and easy to navitate website, and a very satisfied client! If you'd like to view this website please follow this link.
